GHI's STEM and Career Club

The ADO STEM Club is for students who are interested in pursuing STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Math) and want to learn more about future career pathways.

Benefits of Participating

  • Learn about STEM topics like engineering and medicine through engaging projects and field trips, and explore a variety of possible career paths in the sciences
  • Receive volunteer hours for participating in opportunities like tutoring underclassmen/middle-schoolers and organizing STEM club events
  • Hear from career professionals and get advice for participating in extracurricular activities, boosting your resume, and college applications
